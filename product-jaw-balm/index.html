How to use it.

01

Squeeze a pea-sized amount onto two fingers at the end of the day — or whenever the jaw feels held.

02

Locate the masseter: clench briefly and feel the muscle bulge at the angle of your jaw. Unclench, then massage the balm in slow, firm circles for 60–90 seconds each side.

03

Sweep upward to the temples and repeat with lighter pressure. Breathe out as you work — the exhale is part of the release.

04

Pair with the Release Tool for deeper trigger-point work, or finish with a warm compress before bed.

Good questions.

Will this stop my clenching?+
No — and nothing you rub on your skin honestly will. What the balm does is make the muscles that carry your clench feel better: warmth plus massage is a genuinely effective way to release an overworked masseter. For the habit itself, the Day Plate and habit programme do that work; for night protection, the Night Plate.
Is it safe for daily use?+
Yes — it's a cosmetic massage balm formulated for daily use on the jaw, temples and neck. Avoid broken skin and the eye area, and stop if irritation occurs, as with any topical.
Does it smell clinical?+
No — the base is warm and skin-like rather than mentholated. It's designed to be part of a wind-down ritual, not to announce itself across the room.
When will I feel something?+
The warming effect builds within a minute or two of massage and lasts around twenty minutes — which is exactly the window in which slow, firm massage does its best work.
