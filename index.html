Good questions

Asked, answered.

What's the difference between a custom bite plate and a chemist's mouth guard?+
A boil-and-bite guard from the chemist is bulky, loose and one-shape-fits-all — and a loose guard can make clenching worse. An Un-clench plate is custom-made from your own impressions or scan, in a build matched to how hard you grind, and reviewed by a GDC-registered dentist before it's fabricated.
How do I know if I clench my jaw or grind my teeth?+
Common signs are morning headaches at the temples, a sore or tired jaw, worn or sensitive teeth, or a partner hearing grinding at night. Daytime clenchers often catch themselves with their teeth pressed together at a desk. Our free assessment maps your symptoms and tells you which pattern fits.
Do I need to visit a dentist to get one?+
For most people, no. You take impressions at home with our kit — or upload an existing dental scan — and a GDC-registered dentist reviews your case remotely before your plate is made. If your answers suggest something needs an in-person look, we tell you honestly and route you to clinical care.
How long does a custom night guard last?+
Typically 12–18 months depending on how heavily you grind, which is why we match the build to your grinding force — a Soft plate for light-to-moderate clenchers, a clinical-grade Hard build for heavy grinders. Our Refresh subscription keeps your scan on file so replacements are one tap.
How long does delivery take?+
Once we have your impressions or scan and a dentist has approved your case, your plate is fabricated in a UK dental lab and posted to you, typically within 10–14 days.
